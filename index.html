Alex Capecelatro

Alex Capecelatro is an entrepreneur advancing artificial intelligence from research into real-world living environments. As CEO & Co-Founder of Josh.ai, he has led the development of award-winning, privacy-first AI systems that integrate natural language, large language models, and human-centered design into the modern home.

A named inventor on multiple U.S. patents in intelligent systems and interaction design, Capecelatro studied engineering at UCLA and has been recognized for early leadership in applied AI. He has served as Chair of CEDIA and sits on the Board of Integrated Systems Europe (ISE), shaping the global conversation around intelligent environments.

Capecelatro regularly delivers keynote talks at Azione, ProSource, CEDIA, ISE, HTSA, NKBA, and other leading forums on artificial intelligence, design, and the future of connected living.

BEYOND WORK

Outside of technology, Capecelatro is an art collector, runner, skier, pickleball player, and general outdoors enthusiast, with a passion for travel.
