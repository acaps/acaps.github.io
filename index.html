Alex Capecelatro

Alex Capecelatro is an entrepreneur building inspiring, world changing products that harness artificial intelligence for the home. After leaving high school early to work for the U.S. government, he earned an engineering degree from UCLA and conducted research at Sandia National Laboratories, the Naval Research Laboratory, UCLA, and Harvard before beginning his entrepreneurial path. As CEO and Co founder of Josh.ai, he leads the development of award winning, privacy first systems that bring natural language and large language models into everyday living. A named inventor on multiple U.S. patents, he serves on the Boards of CEDIA as Immediate Past Chair, Integrated Systems Europe, and Twenty Summers. He has been recognized on numerous 40 Under 40 lists and regularly speaks on AI, design, and the future of connected living.
