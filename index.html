Alex Capecelatro is the CEO and co founder of Josh.ai, a Denver based artificial intelligence company redefining how people interact with their homes. Founded in 2015, Josh.ai was built around a simple premise: AI in the home must be intelligent, reliable, and privacy conscious.

Under Alex’s leadership, Josh.ai developed a natural language platform designed specifically for residential environments, integrating lighting, climate, audio, security, and more into a cohesive AI experience. The company serves integrators, builders, and homeowners who expect design forward technology that works seamlessly in daily life.

Alex’s work sits at the intersection of AI, hardware, and design. He speaks frequently about the ethical implications of always listening systems, the importance of local processing in smart homes, and the evolution from command based voice control to contextual intelligence.

He serves in leadership roles within the custom integration industry and continues to advocate for thoughtful innovation that prioritizes long term trust over short term data collection.
