Alex Capecelatro

Alex Capecelatro is an entrepreneur advancing artificial intelligence from research into real-world living environments. As CEO & Co-Founder of Josh.ai, he has led the development of award-winning, privacy-first AI systems that bring natural language, large language models, and human-centered design into the modern home. A named inventor on multiple U.S. patents in intelligent systems and interaction design, he has served as Chair of CEDIA and sits on the Board of Integrated Systems Europe (ISE). He regularly speaks at leading industry forums on AI, design, and the future of connected living.
